September 11, 2014





I try to be as descriptive as possible about each item and am pleased to sell quality items. Any defects or imperfections that I see will be described. If the item is vintage, it may not be in "new" condition.  I hope you are pleased with your item and return to my shop often!

Payment



Once payment is received, item will be shipped within 1-3 days.  Once an item is shipped, it cannot be cancelled. If you need to return the item, you are responsible for shipping charges back to me. Please contact me before sending an item back.  If you live in Ohio, I have to charge you sales tax of 6.5%.






Shipping



All apparel items will arrive boxed and/or in a plastic bag inside to assure it arrives in a clean and dry condition.  All home goods items will be wrapped carefully so they arrive intact. If your item arrives damaged, please notify the USPS immediately. Keep all packaging and photograph the damaged contents. Please do not ask me to change my shipping to send it cheaper.  I generally lose money on the shipping due to packaging, gas, travel time, etc.  I ship items so they arrive in nice condition and hate stuffing an item into an envelope that is bulging just to save a couple of bucks.  I will send some smaller items in envelopes if I can be assured they will arrive as I like them to arrive.  If you live out of the country (USA), convo me first to find out what shipping will be, due to varying shipping costs and rules.  I estimated shipping under "Everyone else", so it could be less or more.






Refunds and Exchanges



Returns:  If you need to return an item, please notify me. I usually accept returns in the same condition within 7 days if the item has not met your expectations or if you feel it was not described correctly. Please use the measurements as I do not refund for item not fitting.  You will be credited the amount of the item, less shipping charges and any other fees (Paypal, etc) for any approved return.  You are responsible for shipping charges back to me. Please get approval from me for return before you ship the item back.  

Cancellations:  If you cancel an item before it is shipped, please be aware that I will be charged a fee from Paypal whether or not the order is completed, so that fee amount will not be refunded.  I will process the cancellation within 24 hours of receiving your cancellation request.  Etsy and Paypal have up to 48 hours to process the concellation, so keep checking your account.






Additional policies and FAQs



If you are looking for a specific item, let me know. I will do my best to be on the hunt for it!  I am out at thrifts and sales and auctions and private homes daily and would be glad to keep my eyes open for your treasured wish!
