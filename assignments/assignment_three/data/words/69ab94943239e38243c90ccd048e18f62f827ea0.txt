Shop policies

Last updated on
April 17, 2014





Welcome to my world of color! Color affects me on a deep emotional level and has been known to literally take my breath away. I create luxurious silk scarves and shawls as well as eye-catching jewelry pieces, each a one-of-a-kind creation. Enjoy!
Accepted payment methods


Pay with Visa


Pay with Master Card


Pay with American Express


Pay with Discover


Pay with PayPal







Payment



I currently accept Paypal and all transactions are in US dollars. Items will be shipped after payment has been received. I do not collect any taxes on sales and any sales tax or duty payable at buyer's end is the responsibility of the buyer.






Shipping



All items are shipped within 3-5 business days of purchase. 


I ship all merchandise through Canada Post. Your piece is shipped via surface mail, and usually takes 1-4 weeks, depending on the distance from British Columbia. The size and weight of the package determines the exact service, as Canada Post has restrictions. Your purchase is carefully wrapped and packaged using as much recycled packaging as I am able to source. This helps to keep shipping charges to a minimum.

I don’t make any money on shipping and usually charge my customers what it costs for me to ship their purchase through Canada Post. Please note that I cannot determine beforehand what shipping multiple items will cost. Please note that I give refunds if shipping charges are less than I’ve charged. If it costs more, I pay the difference.


If you would prefer a different shipping method, please contact me. I will be happy to provide you with a price.

I will ship almost anywhere, except where prohibited by law. You are responsible for any duty or taxes payable at the time of delivery. I describe my items accurately to ease the flow through customs.






Refunds and Exchanges



I want you to be happy with your purchase. I do my best to describe each item accurately and point out any perceived flaws. Please contact me if you are not satisfied with your purchase once you receive it or if the package does not arrive. Let's talk about what I can to make it right. 

If color matching is important, please contact me before buying the item. I can send a color swatch and put the item on hold while you decide.






Additional policies and FAQs



I share my home with a dog. Although he is not ever welcome in my studio, the potential exists of cross contamination. If hypo-allergenic is vital, these are probably the wrong choice.
