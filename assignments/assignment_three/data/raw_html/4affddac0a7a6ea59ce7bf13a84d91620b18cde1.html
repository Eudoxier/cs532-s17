CPG Connect is Canada's #1 job board and business networking site exclusively for the Consumer Packaged Goods and Retail industry in Canada.

It is a destination website for professionals and employers in the CPG and Retail industry who regularly use the site to connect, stay current, recruit talent and find that next big job.

Site features include; CPG jobs and retail jobs from over 250 of Canada's top CPG and Retail employers, a detailed company directory of CPG and Retail employers, industry forums by major CPG discipline, networking opportunities with like-minded CPG professionals, an industry vendor directory to find suppliers, industry resources and much more.
