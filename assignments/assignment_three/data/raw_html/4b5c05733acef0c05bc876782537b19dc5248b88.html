HackUCF - Panel - Careers in Infosec

I was delighted to be invited to be a panel speaker at the University of Central Florida.

HackUCF is UCF's Collegiate Cyber Defense Club. They won the national championship the last three years in a row. It was a great panel with a number of my peers and colleagues to help students understand what it takes to get into information security as a career.

You can learn more about the panel here: https://hackucf.org/blog/9232016-2/

Check out HackUCF here: https://hackucf.org/blog/

Check out the National Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition here: http://www.nationalccdc.org/
