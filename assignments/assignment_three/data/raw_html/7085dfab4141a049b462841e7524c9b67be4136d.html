Ajay Saxena - Public Private Partnership Expert - Asian Development Bank

An MBA with Specialization in Information Technology, from Lucknow University. Also a Law Graduate from Lucknow University Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh, and Post Graduate in Economics with specialization in Econometrics from Christ Church College, Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, India.

Currently working with the Asian Development Bank as a PPP (public privatepartnership) - Expert and facilitating the Government of Maharashtra to promote PPP across allthe sectors on the need basis. Major interventions in Institutionalizing of PPP which he has beenworking on are:1. Development of PPP Policy for Government of Maharashtra;2. Draft for PPP Regulator for Maharashtra;3. Setting up a Project Finance Fund for Urban Infrastructure Projects.4. Developing PPP Guidelines and Toolkits for PPP Projects in Water Supply and Sanitation,Urban Transport, Health, Education, City Roads and Solild Waste Management.5. Established Maharashtra’s own Panel of Transaction Advisors to push ProjectDevelopment;6. Rejuvenated Maharashtra Urban Infrastructure Development Company Ltd.7. Strengthening the PPP projects award approval mechanisms.
