OUR MISSION

 

Our mission is simple: make businesses look good at events while providing competitive prices. We built this company because we were fed up with the poor service and high prices of other trade show booth companies. We're proudly located in Orem, Utah, and hope you enjoy working with us! 

FAQ

Do you only sell retractable displays?

No, we sell many other types of displays, besides collapsable booths. Contact us and we'd love to help you find what you're looking for.

How much does printing cost?

Display printing is included in the cost of your display.

I need help with my design, do you offer design services?

Yes. We have experienced designers on our team and would love to help you. Contact us here for design services.

What is the process for purchasing a booth?

This is the process: 1. You select and purchase your desired booth. 2. A PogoExhibits account representative will contact you and provide you with a file to design. 3. You send the finished artwork to your assigned account representative. 4. Your account representative will provide you with a proof to approve. 5. PogoExhibits prints and ships your booth to you. Viola!

How do I give you my artwork?

After purchase, you will be sent the information you need to provide us with your design.
